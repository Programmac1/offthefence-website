About Us

Me -Dan- (Right) and Rob (Left) are two 25-year-olds from Manchester, UK, who have been friends for over 12 years. I am a certified gym bro, history nerd and amateur web developer (can you tell?). Rob is a sports fanatic, music enthusiast and pathological fact-finder. We make each other laugh a fair bit.
By The Fire is a podcast which unpacks the relatable dilemmas, social habits and existential questions that we face as young humans. We like laughing at ourselves and each other, talking about our feelings and figuring out how to improve ourselves.
If this sounds like the podcast for you, you can find our Spotify and Apple Podcast links on the Homepage. You can also stream for free on the Episodes page. We can't wait for you to join the BTF Community.
