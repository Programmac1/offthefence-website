About Us

Dan and Rob

About Your Hosts

Me -Dan- (Right) and Rob (Left) are two 25-year-olds from Manchester, UK, who have been friends for over 12 years. I am a gym addict, history nerd and aspiring software developer. Rob is a sports fanatic, music enthusiast and pathological fact-finder. We make each other laugh a fair bit.

Our Why

Relate Radio is a podcast which explores the weird, wonderful and hilarious ways humans interact with each other. Why are certain situations so awkward and uncomfortable? How do we handle them? How can we not let them knock us back? The social arena is a brutal place sometimes. Let's armor up and get stuck in.

Where To Listen

You can listen to all of our episodes on Spotify and Apple Podcasts or stream them on the Episodes page. You can also stream all of our video episodes on YouTube. New Episodes are uploaded every Sunday at 6pm GMT. Find all of our social media links at the bottom of the website.
